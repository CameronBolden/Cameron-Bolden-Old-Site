About Cameron Bolden

Hello I am Cameron Bolden; I am a graphic designer with a concentration in photography and a minor in advertising. In the next 5 years i want to be one of the top designers around. I’ve played competitive sports since I was in middle school. Being an athlete is one of the reasons I have a strong work ethic. I have a really diverse background which I feel helps with my art. I was a psychology major before I changed to graphic design and I am minoring in advertising as well .I'm a fun outgoing person who strives to be the best I can be. My best two best aspects of design are branding and poster design. When it comes to creating brands and their identity systems I just love when it all comes together to create a cohesive look. Posters are what really got my love for design started.
